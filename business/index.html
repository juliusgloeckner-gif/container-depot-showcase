Questions from buyers

Know before delivery.

Call a container specialist at (855) 525-0902.

Can a container sit behind my business?

Usually, subject to local zoning, fire access and property rules. Confirm permits and clearance with your local authority or landlord.

How much can a 40FT container hold?

A standard 40FT unit provides about 320 square feet of floor space and roughly 2,350 cubic feet of capacity.

Can you add shelving or lighting?

Yes. Ask about shelving, electrical packages, insulation, personnel doors and other modifications.
