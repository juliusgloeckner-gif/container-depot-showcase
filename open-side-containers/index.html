Questions from buyers

Know before delivery.

Call a container specialist at (855) 525-0902.

How many roll-up doors are included?

The featured configuration uses two roll-up doors along the long side of a 20FT container and four along the long side of a 40FT container. Door width and clear opening are confirmed on the fabrication drawing.

Are these factory open-side containers?

These are modified multi-door side-access containers with roll-up doors, not factory full-side bi-fold open-side containers. The standard short-end cargo doors remain, and every new long-side opening is framed and reinforced.

Can the bays be separated?

Yes. Internal partitions, separate locks, shelving, lighting and electrical packages can create dedicated crew, department or inventory zones. Egress and electrical requirements must be reviewed for the intended use.

Are roll-up doors weatherproof and secure?

Commercial roll-up assemblies can be fitted with seals and locks, but performance depends on the selected door, threshold, installation and maintenance. Confirm the required weather resistance and security level before fabrication.
